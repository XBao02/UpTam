ABOUT FUTURETRACK

Our project aims to build a dynamic and intelligent career development platform designed to help students create, improve and manage their CVs throughout their academic journey. From their first year, students can start creating their CVs and continuously update them with new skills, achievements, projects and experiences until graduation. In addition, our platform connects students with job and internship opportunities by allowing companies to post job descriptions and search for suitable candidates. Students can discover job postings that match their skills and career aspirations, while businesses can access a pool of talented candidates through their CVs. To further support career development, the platform includes features such as a networking tool, portfolio showcase and real-time CV feedback from recruiters or career advisors. In the future, AI-powered CV analysis will allow students to upload their existing resumes for instant recommendations and improvements, making the job application process more efficient. By bridging the gap between students and employers, our platform empowers young professionals to confidently enter the job market with strong, well-crafted CVs.
